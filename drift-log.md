## Playbooks page — real content drift

The live `/playbooks/` page was edited directly in WordPress after the initial migration. The local version was never updated to match.

### Live-only copy (added in WP, not in local)

**New Picture Book Project Playbook subtitle:**
> Build and launch a picture book side hustle — every IMPACT step applied to a real build, documented as the work actually happens.

*(Local subtitle was: "Create, publish, and sell a picture book — without guesswork. The training, tools, and tactical support you need to succeed, self publishing your first picture book.")*

**New bulleted feature list under Picture Book Project Playbook:**
- Six chapters — one per IMPACT step
- A Workbook for every chapter
- Built in public and updated as the live build progresses
- Private community access included

**New CTA:**
> Chapter 1 is free — read it before you buy.

### Removed from live (present in local, missing on site)

These sections exist in `local/playbooks.html` but were stripped from the live WP version:

- **"How it works"** explainer block — the Strategy / Tactics / Execution hierarchy
- **"Inside a playbook"** diagram and copy — the Step 1 → Identify Workbook → Worksheets flow
- **"Inside a Workbook"** stages explanation
- **"Not a finished course. A live document."** pull quote

### Open decision

Was the live simplification **intentional** (drop the explainers, keep the page lean) or **accidental** (WP/Cadence rebuild regression)?
- If intentional → adopt the simplified Playbooks page for the rebuild.
- If accidental → keep the richer local version and layer in the live-only additions above.
