Inside a playbook

"Not a finished course.
A live document."

Every playbook follows the same structure: six chapters, one per IMPACT step, applied to a specific business type. The difference is how it's made — each one is worked through in real time. The decisions documented here aren't theoretical. You're following what is actually being built.


Every chapter comes with a Workbook — a structured collection of worksheets for every task in that step. The Worksheets are where the work actually gets done.
