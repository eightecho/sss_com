The IMPACT System — The Playbook Library

The framework,
in action.

The IMPACT System is universal. A Playbook makes it specific — every step already applied to one business archetype, with every decision documented as the build actually happens.

What a Playbook Is

The IMPACT System gives you the six steps. A Playbook shows you those six steps applied — to a real business type, by someone actually building it, in real time.

Each Playbook is built around a single archetype: a specific creator, a specific product category, and the specific constraints that come with building alongside a primary job. You're not following a template. You're following a build — with all the choices already made and the reasoning on the page.

  • Training: Six chapters — one per IMPACT step — applied to the specific business type. Real decisions, documented as they're made.
  • Tools: A task-specific worksheet for every chapter. Structured exercises that force the decisions each step requires. You finish each chapter with something to show for it.
  • Community: Private community access included. Ask questions, share progress, and follow the live build as it continues.
Live Builds

Every Playbook in the Stupid Simple Startup Program is built in public — documented chapter by chapter as the business is actually being constructed. Readers don't get a finished retrospective. They get a live record, updated as the build progresses.
