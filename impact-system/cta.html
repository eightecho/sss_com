The IMPACT System — Niche Playbook

You've read the system.
Here's the playbook.

The IMPACT System gives you the framework. The Picture Book Project Playbook gives you the decisions already made — every step applied to the specific challenge of building and launching a picture book project alongside a primary job.

What the Playbook Covers

The IMPACT System is universal. The Picture Book Project Playbook is specific. It walks through all six steps with the choices, timelines, and decision criteria calibrated for one archetype: a creator with a picture book idea, a primary job, limited time, and no interest in guessing.

Every chapter answers the same questions the IMPACT System raises — but for your context, with your constraints, at your pace.

  • Identify applied: How to define your picture book's audience, problem, and positioning in a crowded market — before you write a word.
  • Monetize applied: The economics of self-publishing a picture book. Revenue models, realistic unit economics, and pricing strategy for a physical and digital product.
  • Productize applied: Version strategy for a picture book project. What MVP looks like, what v1 requires, and how to scope the project so it actually ships.
  • Activate applied: The minimum infrastructure a picture book creator needs — and nothing more. Base Camp, lead capture, and the content channel that makes sense for this audience.
  • Campaign applied: How to run a launch campaign for a picture book. Pre-sale strategy, community activation, and the campaign structure that fits a small but engaged audience.
  • Triumph applied: How to analyze launch results, decide what comes next, and set up the loop for a second project informed by the first.
  • Workbook access: The companion workbook set — one per step — forces the decisions and calculations each stage requires.
