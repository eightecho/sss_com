What a playbook is

The IMPACT System,
already applied.

The IMPACT System tells you what to do at each step. A playbook shows you how it was actually done — every tool selected, every decision made, every shortcut learned — for one specific type of business.


The Picture Book Project Playbook applies all six steps to building and launching a picture book side hustle alongside a primary job. It's not theory. It's a live document — updated as the actual build progresses.

Inside the playbook

Training. Tools. Community.
