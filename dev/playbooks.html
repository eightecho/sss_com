How it works

The hierarchy

Strategy. Tactics. Execution.

Three layers. Each one builds on the last.

Strategy

The IMPACT System

A six-step framework for turning a side hustle into a real business. The strategy applies to any business type, at any stage. Always free.

Tactics

The Playbook

Each playbook takes the IMPACT System and applies it to a specific business type — every decision, every tool, every deliverable documented in real time as the work happens.

Execution

Workbooks & Worksheets

Every chapter has a Workbook — a structured collection of worksheets for every task in that step. Included with every Playbook, along with community access.
