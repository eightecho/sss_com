The IMPACT System™

One Powerful Framework

IMPACT

Six steps. One sequence.

A free, complete framework for building a business from scratch. Do the steps in order. The work gets simpler when you stop guessing what comes next.

Step 1 · Identify

Validate before you build.

Lock in the idea, understand the customer, and confirm real demand — before you invest time or money in the wrong direction.

Step 2 · Monetize

Price before you produce.

Build a real revenue model before you create a single deliverable. Price, packaging, and proof of demand come first.

Step 3 · Productize

Build what you've already sold.

Create the actual thing — scoped, focused, and shippable. Done is the goal. Perfect is not.

Step 4 · Activate

Get first sales without a launch.

Revenue before the audience, the ad spend, or the big launch event. First customers through direct effort.

Step 5 · Campaign

Build the sales engine.

Develop the distribution and audience systems that generate ongoing, repeatable revenue long after launch.

Step 6 · Triumph

Review, decide, and repeat.

Close the loop. What worked, what didn't, and what comes next — documented and decided before moving on.
