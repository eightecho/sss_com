Step 1 Worksheet

Identify

Target Audience

  • Define your primary reader — age range, reading level, and format (board book, picture book, early reader)
  • Identify the buyer — who actually makes the purchase? (parent, grandparent, teacher, librarian)
  • List 3 adjacent audiences your book could serve beyond the primary reader

Market Research

  • Find 5 comparable titles on Amazon — record title, author, price, review count, and BSR
  • Note the categories each title ranks in and whether the niche is saturated or accessible
  • Identify the gap — what are reviewers asking for that the top titles don't deliver?

Questions

Common questions

Do I need to have a finished idea before I start?

You need a general direction — "I want to make a picture book about [topic]" — but that's all. Chapter 1 (Identify) is specifically designed to help you validate and sharpen the idea before you commit to anything. You don't need a finished concept to get started.

Do I need to be able to illustrate?

No. Chapter 3 (Productize) covers exactly how to find, brief, and work with an illustrator — including what to look for, how to structure the agreement, and how to manage the process. Your job is the concept and story. Someone else handles the artwork.

What is the community, and how does it work?

It's a private community hosted on Heartbeat. Access is included when you purchase the playbook. Ask questions at any step, share your progress, and get direct feedback as you work through each chapter.

Is the playbook complete, or is it still being documented?

Both. The playbook is a live document — each step is being actively worked through in real time. Chapters are documented and released as the work is completed. When you purchase, you get immediate access to everything that's been released, plus all future chapters as they're added.

Do I need the free IMPACT System first?

Not required, but recommended. The IMPACT System explains the full six-step framework at a high level — understanding it first gives context that makes each chapter of the playbook sharper. It's free and takes about 20 minutes to read.

Is this only for picture books, or can I use it for other types of books?

This playbook is built specifically for picture books — every step, every example, and every worksheet is picture-book-specific. The Productize chapter especially goes deep on picture book production. If you're self-publishing a different type of book, the underlying IMPACT System framework still applies, but this playbook won't be a perfect fit.
